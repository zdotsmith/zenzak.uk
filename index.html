FAQ

Your questions, animated

Quick answers to common questions about my design and animation process.

What can you bring to life?

From photorealistic product renders to dynamic animations of complex machinery, I specialize in turning technical concepts into clear, compelling visuals.

How do we get started?

Our process is simple and transparent. We'll start with a discovery call to align on your goals. From there, I'll manage the project from initial concept to final render, providing you with key updates at every milestone.

What do you need from me?

Your vision is the starting point. CAD files, technical specs, photos, or even a simple sketch are all perfect. The more detail you can provide, the more precise and impactful the final visualization will be.

How long does it take?

Most projects are completed within 1–3 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. You will receive a clear timeline with your project quote. If you have an urgent deadline, let's discuss it.

Can you animate my existing designs?

Absolutely. I can work directly with your existing engineering files, as well as photos or sketches, to create stunning and technically accurate animations.

How much does it cost?

Every project is unique, so pricing is custom-quoted to fit your specific requirements. You will receive a detailed, transparent quote upfront—no hidden fees, just exceptional results.
